About the Artist

Hi I'm Kayt! I started Kayt's Blend as a way to collect all of the hobbies I have picked up in my 25 years. I love creating, in any and every way, so I have plenty of art and handcraft to share.

If you're interested in starting any of these hobbies yourself, I've added KB Tools as a resource. As my list of hobbies grows, so will the articles for tools and tips! I hope to share with you the most useful tools for beginners, and save you the unnecessary frustration of trial and error. KB Tools also features fun creativity related articles, written or curated by me. Happy reading!

Thank you for supporting my small business, I hope that my page inspires your own creativity!
