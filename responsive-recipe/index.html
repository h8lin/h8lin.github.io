This is not a Greek salad, but it is as close as I can get while being fairly picky and with a lot of food sensitivities. This is my favorite because it is great for nights when I want dinner to be fresh but also warm--plus I also really love Greek food.
Tzatziki Style Sauce

Tzatziki Style Sauce

  1. Ingredients
    • ¾ cup Chopped Cucumber
    • 1 cup Plain Greek Yogurt
    • 1 tbsp Fresh Dill (or ½ tbsp Dried Dill)
    • Salt and Pepper (to taste)
    • ½ tbsp Garlic Infused Oil*
    • ½ Lemon, juiced and zested
    • Optional: 2-3 cloves of discarded garlic cloves from oil
  2. To prepare
    • Add to a bowl and mix until combined. Alternatively, it can be blended in a blender or food processor if you prefer less texture.

*Add a handful (about 5-10) of garlic cloves to 1 cup of olive oil and heat on low for 15 minutes, let cool and remove cloves. Store in the fridge for about 2 weeks.

Salad

Salad

  1. Ingredients
    • About 1 ½ cup of Diced Cucumber
    • 10-15 halved Cherry Tomatoes
    • 1 diced Red Bell Pepper
    • 1 diced Orange or Yellow Bell Pepper
    • 1 tbsp Fresh Dill (or ½ tbsp Dried Dill)
    • ½ Lemon, juiced and zested
    • Feta for garnish
  2. To prepare
    • Add cucumber, cherry tomatoes, and peppers to a bowl. Mix in half of your lemon juice and zest, salt and pepper, and dill then taste. Add more lemon as desired. Top with sauce from the previous step and crumbled feta.
Mini Pita

Mini Pita

  1. Ingredients
    • Mini Pita (or just Pita)
    • Garlic Infused Oil
    • Salt
    • 1 tsp Fresh Dill (or ½ tsp Dried Dill)
  2. To prepare
    • Heat large pan on medium heat. Drizzle garlic infused oil over mini pita bread (or regular sized pita) and place oil side down on the hot pan for about 30 seconds or until browned and slightly crispy. Top with salt and dill. Best served warm to dip in sauce or top with salad.
